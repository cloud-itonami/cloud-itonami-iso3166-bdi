# Business model — BDI

Independent public-sector market-entry compliance for Burundi.

- Autorité de Régulation des Marchés Publics (ARMP) e-procurement /
  notice publication (armp.gov.bi) -- with prior/posterior contrôle
  split out to the Direction Nationale de Contrôle des Marchés Publics
  (DNCMP) and passation itself run by each Autorité Contractante's own
  Cellule de Gestion des Marchés Publics (CGMP).
- Registre du Commerce et des Sociétés (RCS) + NIF (Office Burundais
  des Recettes), bundled since 2021 into the ADB-operated
  easybusiness.bi single window.

Trust Controls: any actual portal registration or filing submission
requires Market-Entry Compliance Governor clearance and always
escalates to human sign-off; a false or fabricated regulatory-
requirement claim is a HARD hold.
